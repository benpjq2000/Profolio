Proposal Summary

This research aims to build a graph-attention network model used to predict muti-risk based on large genome-wide data. Genome plays a critical role in the biological study and it is an important aspect of disease prediction. Since multi-risk disease prediction has not been studied, utilizing the advantages of the graph-attention network can improve the effectiveness and accuracy of multi-risk disease prediction using large genomic data. This research will provide more comprehensive treatment plans, leading to a higher survival rate for humans.
